At Mybhumi, we believe that good food is an essential part of any great vacation. That's why we have a farm-to-table restaurant that serves delicious local cuisine made with fresh ingredients sourced from our farm and the surrounding region.

Our restaurant offers a range of traditional dishes prepared by our expert chefs. We believe in using locally sourced and organic ingredients wherever possible to ensure the freshest and most delicious meals. Our menu changes regularly to reflect the seasons and the availability of fresh produce.

Our dining area is designed to provide a relaxed and comfortable atmosphere, with stunning views of the surrounding hills and greenery. You can choose to dine indoors or outdoors, depending on your preference and the weather. Overall, our restaurant is the perfect place to relax and unwind after a day of exploring the beautiful Junnar region. Come and enjoy delicious food, stunning views, and warm hospitality at Mybhumi.
